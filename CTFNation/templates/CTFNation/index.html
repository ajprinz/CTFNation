About

CTFNation is a platform designed for hackers to compete against others around the world within minutes, unlike regular capture the flag events. Simply choose one of the many different game types to compete in, get paired with a team generated by our custom algorithms, our servers will then create a custom map from a massive database of vulnerabilities found in the wild, and once your map loads it’s no holds barred as you hack your way to victory. Each win will progress your rank and your spot on the leaderboard as hackers fight to join the elite at the top.
